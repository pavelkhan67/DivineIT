Industry Recognition

Since 2005, Adventure Soft Ltd. has been developing solutions for businesses in different industries. In these 18 years, the company has reached more than 65 industries that are served by Divine IT’s consultancy services, development and implementation of web applications.

Through constant enhancement of features and development of technology, PrismERP (flagship product of Adventure Soft Ltd.) has achieved BASIS National ICT Awards 2017, APICTA International Awards 2017 and attained the highest recognition locally by achieving National Productivity & Quality Excellence Award twice for contributing in the small and medium industries of Bangladesh.
